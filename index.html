Journey of our Team!

Hello everyone! We are Milz Esports, and I am delighted that you have visited our websites. We first met in high school when we were in the same class. We didn't start playing at the same time because we each have different interests. So, when a pandemic strikes, the only thing we can do is play games, so we play and play until we plan to enter any online competition. We practiced and practiced until we were stronger, and now we each have many accomplishments. When the pandemic is over, we intend to participate in a face-to-face tournament in which we will occasionally win and occasionally lose. When we arrived at college, the coact student council announced a mobile legends tournament, which we intend to attend. When tournament time comes, we dominate any single game and become champion, and we are ready to defend our title this year.
